ABOUT CORI

Cori is an astrologist based in Asheville, NC. She has been practicing for ten years and has recently begun to hold one-on-one consultations, even during the COVID-19 pandemic. Her fascination with astrology grew from a preoccupation with the mystery of life and the wonder of learning about yourself. Now, she is able to share it with others through comprehensive and detailed analysis.
